About ParaDetect AI

Our Mission

ParaDetect AI is an advanced deep learning system designed to revolutionize malaria diagnosis through automated microscopic blood smear analysis. Our mission is to provide healthcare professionals, especially in resource-limited settings, with a fast, accurate, and accessible tool for malaria parasite detection.

Malaria remains one of the world's most devastating diseases, affecting millions annually. Traditional microscopic examination requires expert pathologists and is time-consuming, creating barriers to timely diagnosis. Our AI-powered solution addresses these challenges by providing instant, reliable screening results.

How It Works

Image Upload

Upload microscopic blood smear images in standard formats (PNG, JPG, JPEG). Our system accepts high-resolution images for optimal analysis.

AI Analysis

Advanced Convolutional Neural Network processes the image, trained on thousands of blood smear samples to identify malaria parasites.

Results

Receive instant classification results with confidence scores and probability distributions for informed medical decision-making.

Training Data

Our model is trained on the comprehensive NIH Malaria Dataset, containing over 27,000 cell images with expert annotations. The dataset includes:

  • 13,779 parasitized cell images
  • 13,779 uninfected cell images
  • High-resolution microscopic images
  • Expert pathologist annotations
  • Diverse geographic and demographic representation

Privacy & Security

We prioritize patient privacy and data security. All uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after analysis. The system complies with healthcare data protection standards and ensures complete confidentiality of medical information.
